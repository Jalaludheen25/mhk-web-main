About M.H.K Trading & Ship Chandlers

Building maritime excellence since 2014 with unwavering commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Excellence in Maritime Supply & Trading

M.H.K Trading & Ship Chandlers LLC stands as a beacon of reliability in the maritime industry. Founded in 2014 by visionary entrepreneur Hariharasudan Gnanasekaran, our company has evolved from a local ship chandling service to a comprehensive maritime and industrial solutions provider.

With over 14 years of dedicated service, we have built an unparalleled reputation for delivering exceptional quality products, innovative solutions, and reliable services to the maritime, construction, and industrial sectors across the UAE and beyond.
